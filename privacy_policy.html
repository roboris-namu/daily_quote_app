Privacy Policy for Daily Quotes

Last updated: September 6, 2024

1. Information We Collect

Daily Quotes is a simple, offline-first application. We do not collect any personal information from users. The app operates entirely on your device without sending data to external servers.

2. Data Storage

All data, including your favorite quotes and language preferences, is stored locally on your device using the device's local storage. No data is transmitted to our servers or third parties.

3. Permissions

Daily Quotes may request the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your favorite quotes locally
  • Share: To allow you to share quotes with other apps

4. Third-Party Services

Daily Quotes does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app is completely self-contained.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal information from children or adults.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: dailyquotes@example.com

This privacy policy is effective as of the date listed above and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future.
